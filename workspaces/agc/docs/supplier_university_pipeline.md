Why:
- consulting is the chosen lead lane for the next year
- the strongest current proof is in education program management and operations

## University Onboarding Playbook

Organized from the raw process notes.

### Standard sequence

1. Get registered in the university vendor system.
2. If there is no registration system, find the procurement office and buyer contacts.
3. Start with known AGC educational-branch notes, then verify on the university website.
4. Call the main procurement office when buyer names are missing.
5. Nurture the relationship:
   - vendor events
   - email
   - calls
   - follow-up meetings
6. Organize what bids, quotes, or categories the institution may need.
7. Confirm documentation requirements for that institution.
8. Execute on supplier/partner transaction if there is a real fit.
9. Repeat with a cleaner record each cycle.

### Common documentation to centralize

The raw notes imply AGC needs one reusable document kit plus institution-specific add-ons.

Common kit should likely include:
- business information sheet
- W-9
- capability statement
- contact sheet
- registration numbers and certifications
- banking/remit information when needed
- insurance documents when required

Institution-specific add-ons may include:
- vendor portal forms
- commodity or category selection
- bidder lists
- diversity/supplier forms
- institution-specific compliance or onboarding documents
