## Working Recommendation

### 1. Lead market for the next 90 days

`Universities and education-adjacent institutional buyers` should lead first.

Why:
- Johnnie's strongest current proof is in education program launches, admissions operations, CRM/reporting, and process improvement.
- The AGC raw archive already contains the deepest researched target list for universities.
- Universities create a more honest first lane for `operational clarity` and `workflow modernization` than jumping straight to federal primes or agencies with no current certifications and no live pipeline.
- Federal and prime opportunities should still be surfaced, but they should be treated as `secondary / opportunistic` until AGC rebuilds motion and proof.

### 2. Lead sales motion

The first motion should be:

`consulting-led outreach into universities and institutional buyers, while keeping products/sourcing as a secondary vertical rather than the main entry point.`

Why:
- consulting is the chosen lead business
- education program management is the strongest proof lane
- supplier readiness is currently unverified
- product sourcing can re-enter once supplier access and target demand are re-confirmed

### 3. Fastest honest path

The fastest honest path is:

`direct relationship-building with university and institutional buyers first, not blind bidding at scale.`

Practical translation:
- register where needed
- identify buyers and internal champions
- start direct outreach
- capture live interest
- then respond to fit-aligned bid or quote opportunities

This is a better first move than trying to brute-force federal bidding from a cold start.
