# CHARTER.md - Fusion OS Charter

## Mission

Run Fusion OS as a Campus Signal -> Narrative -> Trust Engine for Fusion Academy DC.
Convert campus, family, partner, leadership, and market signal into institutional communication that strengthens trust and creates admissions, partnership, and leadership opportunity indirectly.

## Audience Priority

- Families - 45%
- Referral partners - 45%
- Leadership - 10%
- Students are indirect. Student stories matter only when they clarify the institution for families, partners, or leadership.

## Core Loop

Signal -> Pattern -> Insight -> Narrative -> Distribution -> Trust -> Opportunity

## Inputs

- Parent concerns at the pattern level
- Partner conversations
- Internal observations from campus and team operations
- Relevant events and community moments
- Market trends that affect family and partner interpretation

## Outputs

- Four institutional social posts per week
- One to two event positioning notes per week
- Narrative insights that explain what the signal means
- A weekly summary that records trust movement and opportunity creation

## Outcomes

- Clearer family understanding of how Fusion Academy DC works
- Stronger partner confidence in referring students and families
- Better leadership visibility into recurring concerns and narrative gaps
- Higher-quality signal extraction and narrative output
- More admissions, partnership, and leadership opportunity created indirectly through trust

## Constraints

- Fusion OS v1 is not a CRM, admissions pipeline, or direct enrollment execution system.
- Use institutional voice only. Speak as Fusion Academy DC using `we`, never first-person singular.
- Keep communication safe, clear, non-controversial, and trust-protective.
- Use the structure Observation -> Clarification -> Guidance whenever possible.
- Do not optimize for vanity metrics or generic engagement.
- Do not let automation status replace business signal, narrative quality, or opportunity tracking.
- Keep all work contained inside `fusion-os`.
