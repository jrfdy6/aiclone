# Fusion OS Operating Model

## Definition

Fusion OS v1 is a Campus Signal -> Narrative -> Trust Engine for Fusion Academy DC.
It does not run admissions execution directly.
It improves admissions, partnerships, and leadership outcomes indirectly by producing better institutional communication.

## Audience System

- Families - 45%
- Referral partners - 45%
- Leadership - 10%

Students are indirect.
Student experience matters because it helps families and partners understand the institution, not because Fusion OS is a student-expression lane.

## Core Loop

1. Signal
   Gather meaningful input from parent concerns, partner conversations, internal observations, events, and market trends.
2. Pattern
   Identify repetition, confusion, emerging concerns, or narrative gaps.
3. Insight
   State what the pattern means in plain institutional terms.
4. Narrative
   Turn the insight into a clear point of view that Fusion Academy DC can stand behind publicly.
5. Distribution
   Publish the narrative through weekly posts, event notes, and summaries.
6. Trust
   Watch whether the audience better understands the school and its role.
7. Opportunity
   Record what new referral, family conversation, leadership alignment, or partnership opening became possible.

## Inputs

- Parent concerns at the pattern level
- Partner conversations and referral feedback
- Internal observations from campus and team operations
- Relevant school, city, education, and community events
- Market trends that change how families or partners interpret private education

## Outputs

- 4 posts per week
- Event positioning notes
- Narrative insights
- Weekly summary

## Content System

Weekly content split:

- 2 family posts
- 2 partner posts

Primary content pillars:

- Family Clarity
- Partner Credibility
- Behind-the-Scenes
- Leadership POV

## Voice System

All public-facing output must:

- use institutional voice
- speak as Fusion Academy DC using `we`
- avoid first-person singular
- stay safe, clear, and non-controversial
- follow Observation -> Clarification -> Guidance

## Event Engine

Each week the system must:

- identify 1 to 2 relevant events,
- frame why each event matters before it happens,
- and extract a usable insight after it happens.

The point of event work is not event coverage for its own sake.
The point is to use real moments to sharpen institutional narrative.

## Opportunity Definition

An opportunity is created when trust produces one of these:

- a better family conversation,
- a stronger partner referral opening,
- a clearer leadership decision,
- or a more credible institutional position in the market.

## Non-Goals

- direct admissions pipeline management
- enrollment operations ownership
- personal-brand content
- reactive trend chasing
- automation work with no narrative or audience consequence
