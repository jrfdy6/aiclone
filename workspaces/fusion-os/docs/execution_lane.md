# Fusion OS Execution Lane

Fusion OS executes one recurring business loop:

Signal -> Pattern -> Insight -> Narrative -> Distribution -> Trust -> Opportunity

Automation, PM cards, SOPs, and write-back exist to support this loop.
They are not the loop itself.

## Purpose

Turn weekly campus and market signal into institutional narrative output that improves trust with families and referral partners while giving leadership a clearer read on what the institution needs to explain.

## Lane Responsibilities

- Capture and cluster signal from families, partners, internal observations, events, and market trends
- Decide which signals are worth public narrative and which stay internal
- Produce four posts per week in institutional voice
- Frame one to two relevant events each week before they happen
- Extract insight from those events after they happen
- Record weekly audience response and opportunities created

## Primary Audiences

- Families - 45%
- Referral partners - 45%
- Leadership - 10%

## Valid Work Types

- Signal review
- Narrative development
- Family post production
- Partner post production
- Event positioning
- Post-event insight extraction
- Weekly summary writing
- Standup preparation tied to signal, content, response, and opportunity

## Invalid Work Types

- Direct admissions pipeline management
- CRM maintenance
- Enrollment operations ownership
- Automation validation as the primary deliverable
- Generic engagement chasing
- Personal-brand content

## Minimum Weekly Deliverables

- 2 family posts
- 2 partner posts
- 1 to 2 event selections
- Pre-event framing for each selected event
- Post-event insight extraction when an event produced usable signal
- 1 weekly summary

## Definition of Done For A Weekly Cycle

A weekly Fusion cycle is complete when:

- the required four posts are produced,
- the event engine has been run,
- the most important signals have been translated into clear narrative,
- audience response has been reviewed,
- opportunity created by trust has been recorded,
- and the next narrative focus is explicit.

## Support Layer

PM cards, SOPs, workspace briefings, execution logs, and standups should describe the work above in traceable form.
They should not become the main subject of the lane unless a system failure blocks output or visibility.

## Escalation Rule

Escalate only when one of these is true:

- signal quality is too weak to support safe communication,
- institutional voice or trust constraints are unclear,
- an event or market change requires leadership interpretation,
- or automation failure prevents the weekly output from being produced or tracked.
