# CHARTER.md - FEEZIE OS Charter

## Mission

Run the public-facing operating system for Feeze's visibility, starting with LinkedIn and expanding over time into a broader personal-brand and career-positioning lane.

## Outcomes

- Stronger thought leadership rooted in real work
- Better alignment between lived work, public signal, and long-term positioning
- Reusable visibility assets from reactions, source signals, drafts, and operating lessons
- A clean path from LinkedIn-first execution to full FEEZIE OS across future surfaces
- Public-facing signal that tells Feeze's story more clearly than ad hoc posting ever could

## Constraints

- No invented stories or fake lived experience.
- No public posting without owner approval.
- Persona truth beats surface engagement tactics.
- Live source signals beat generic ideation when both are available.

## Anti-Goals

FEEZIE OS should never become:

- a generic autoposter
- AI slop with no lived proof
- vanity content disconnected from real work
- engagement hacking that damages trust
- a lane that flattens Feeze into one-dimensional "AI founder" branding

## Horizon Markers

### 30 Days

- The lane is integrated into the daily workflow instead of being an occasional side effort.
- Signal intake, reactions, and draft creation are consistent and grounded in real work.
- The public voice is recognizably Feeze, not generic content tooling.

### 90 Days

- The system reliably turns lived operating work into reusable public assets.
- LinkedIn cadence supports business credibility, career positioning, and a clearer public narrative.
- Canonical persona, standups, PM flow, and workspace execution reinforce each other instead of drifting.

### 180 Days

- FEEZIE OS operates as a true public visibility system, not just a LinkedIn posting workflow.
- The lane supports a broader personal brand across career, leadership, AI, operations, education, referral, therapy, entrepreneurship, and style without collapsing them into one voice.
- The system has enough memory and signal quality to tell Feeze's story coherently over time.
