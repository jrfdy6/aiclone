# Easy Outfit App Standup Contract

## Purpose

Easy Outfit App standups exist to review the health of the product and recommendation loop.
They do not exist to center runner churn or automation status unless those issues block execution or visibility.

## Required Standup Structure

Every Easy Outfit App standup must use these five sections in this order:

1. Signal
2. Work Produced
3. Traction
4. Opportunities
5. Next Focus

## Section Definitions

### 1. Signal

Report:

- the most important product, user, or context signal captured this period
- where it came from
- and what it suggests about recommendation quality or product health

### 2. Work Produced

Report:

- what product, metadata, recommendation, or restoration artifact changed
- what user problem it supported
- and whether the artifact is ready, shipped, or still blocked

### 3. Traction

Report:

- what happened with `website visits` or product use
- whether the signal is usable or still too thin
- and what changed in trust, clarity, or product usefulness

Do not confuse aesthetic preference with trustworthy traction.

### 4. Opportunities

Report:

- what product, onboarding, or user-behavior opening became visible
- why it matters
- and what should happen next

### 5. Next Focus

Report:

- the next product or recommendation-quality move
- whether the lane is in restore mode or grow mode
- the blocker that most threatens trustworthy progress

## Standup Rules

- Keep the business loop visible: Wardrobe Signal -> Context -> Recommendation -> Visit or Use -> Feedback -> Improve.
- Keep the product closet-first and context-aware.
- Do not let affiliate-style purchase pressure replace actual product utility.
- Mention automation only when it blocks output, tracking, or decision quality.
- Keep the lane isolated to `easyoutfitapp`.

## Good Standup Outcome

A good Easy Outfit App standup makes it obvious:

- what the product is learning
- what changed in the app or recommendation system
- whether visits or use are improving
- what product opportunity is now visible
- and what the next trustworthy move should be
