# Nightly Self-Improvement Skill

## Steps
1. **Collect Inputs**
   - Inspect cron logs, doc-updates, and `memory/cron-prune.md` for the past day.
   - Note successes/failures in automations, heartbeats, or deploys.
2. **Analyze Automations**
   - Identify what worked, what failed, and what needs tuning.
3. **Write Lessons**
   - Append insights to `memory/self-improvement.md` (date-stamped bullet list).
4. **Propose Tweaks**
   - Suggest at least two actionable adjustments for tomorrow (e.g., prompt change, schedule shift).
5. **Report**
   - Summarize findings + proposed tweaks in the outgoing message (Discord channel `1482486716584689856`).

## Template
```
Nightly Self-Improvement — <date>
Wins:
- ...
Frictions:
- ...
Tomorrow’s Tweaks:
1. ...
2. ...
```
